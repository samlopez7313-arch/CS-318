About Berserk

Berserk is a dark fantasy manga about Guts, a powerful mercenary known as the Black Swordsman, who seeks revenge and sanctuary from demonic forces after a traumatic event known as the Eclipse. The story, set in a brutal medieval world, explores themes of fate, ambition, betrayal, and the struggle for humanity against a backdrop of war, monsters, and a cosmic conspiracy. Guts' journey involves his past with the ambitious mercenary leader Griffith and his love, Casca, and his ongoing fight against the supernatural beings called the God Hand and their Apostles.

Main Character Overview

  • Guts: The main protagonist, a lone mercenary with a tragic past and a relentless drive for revenge.
  • Griffith: The charismatic leader of the Band of the Hawk and Guts' former friend, whose ambition leads to catastrophic events.
  • Casca: A skilled warrior and Guts' love interest, who struggles with her own trauma and the consequences of their choices.

Various Berserk Media

Berserk has been adapted into several media formats, including a 1997 and 2016 anime, the manga (that most media adaptations are based on), and video games. Each adaptation offers a unique perspective on the story and characters.
