Ham and Bananas Hollandaise

width="500">

Ingredients

  • 6 medium bananas
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 6 thin slices boiled ham
  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 envelopes hollandaise sauce mix
  • 1/4 cup light cream

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Lightly butter 2-quart, shallow baking dish.
  3. Peel bananas; sprinkle each with 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice, to prevent darkening.
  4. Spread ham slices with mustard. Wrap each banana in slice of ham. Arrange in single layer in casserole. Bake 10 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, make sauce: In small saucepan, combine sauce mix with 1 cup water, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, and cream. Heat, stirring, to boiling; pour over bananas. Bake 5 minutes longer, or until slightly golden. Nice with a green salad for brunch or lunch
